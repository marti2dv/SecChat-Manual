Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use SecChat over other chat options?

SecChat is a peer-to-peer network, meaning no one owns the server that your chat is hosted on. The chat records stay only on the computers in a given chat room. This makes SecChat good for people who are worried about security.

What makes SecChat secure?

SecChat uses encryption to hide what you are sending from eavesdroppers. SecChat also uses specialized routing to hide who various users are communicating with.

What encryption does SecChat use?

SecChat currently uses RSA public key cryptography. Wikipedia has a good explanation of RSA encryption that can be seen here if interested.

How are messages sent through the SecChat network?

SecChat uses our own custom built network based on Kademlia, which is a peer-to-peer system for distributed storage. The academic paper describing Kademlia can found here.
